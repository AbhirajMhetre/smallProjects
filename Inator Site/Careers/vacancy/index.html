    UX Designer

    We are expanding our brilliant UX team of five with someone who has a couple of years of experience in digital product related stuff. Our clients range from small to international. So, if the description below suits you, your application is more than welcome!

    Your tasks will be...

    Information architecture and interface design.

    Prepare and design different web and mobile based projects (websites, portals, landing pages, online applications or whatever comes to our clients’ mind).

    Create wireframes, customer journeys for use cases.

    Design content and feature concepts.

    Documentation and specifications. Being tidy and well-organised is important.

    Competitor analysis, discovering best practices to be up to date and also user-friendly.

    Usability testing with different methods and tools in order to make customers, business, and us happy.

    What we Expect from you...

    Being precise and putting excellent quality work on the table is a no-brainer. It is also important that we expect this not only from you, but each and every other member of the team and the client as well.

    You have empathy to discover, understand and keep at top priority the users’ needs.

    You create clear and unambiguous wireframes that anyone can work with.

    You have a natural curiosity towards UX - from scrolling through professional groups to having goals for the next few years in this domain. Hint: we will help you along the way to reach your dreams.

    Clear and transparent communication is not only essential but a must. You are fluent in written and oral English.

    We are going to be exceptionally happy if you...

    Have been using Figma for a while now and also enjoy using it.

    Have agency understanding and experience.

    Are experienced in product design or service design.

    Have a portfolio showing what types of professional challenges you have faced before.

    Have tamed a raging bear using only kind words - actually that’s a huge plus, hopefully you won’t need to use it.
