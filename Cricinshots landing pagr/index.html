About App

A visual representation of cricket that will make you ponder the pace of cricket.
An Augmented reality, real-time, immersive Cricketing Experience.

In a world of fast action and lives lived in the fast lane, why be stuck with the slow pace of cricket on TV or the dreary text of Cricket Commentary? What if you could see and understand, in a glance, what each ball in a game means, no reading text or waiting for players to move. Follow the action at your own pace and be freed of the anchor that is a TV or match video.

The CricInShots app visualizes each ball in a game to get you a picture of the field and the action on it. In a few seconds and a single glance, you will know how many runs, where the ball went and who was on the pitch.
