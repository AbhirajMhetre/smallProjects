    About Us

    The Journey

    We have just begun but these are our little steps. Afterall, the journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.

    • 2019

      An Agency is Born

      Inator LLP was Co-Founded by Harsh Savergaonkar, Kunal Raje and Manas Sambhus in 2019. Harsh was the visionary, Kunal the realist and Manas the backbone. The original name was supposed to be IndevInfinity, but a few minutes before signing the documents, Harsh and Kunal changed it to ‘Inator’ at the last moment based on a stupid whim.

    • March 2020

      The Pandemic impact

      Inator was formed just for CricInShots(our then primary product), but Covid-19 postponed the launch of CricInShots by months and hence we got into a lot of other things and now CricInShots is a separate child company of Inator.

    • Feb 2019 – August 2020

      Phase Two Expansion

      We got Incubated by MIT TBI! We finally had an office / a workplace! We got our very first batch of interns – Team 1.0! Full fledged work started on CricInShots

    • July 2020

      Transition to Full Service:

      Most of our products need massive investments, server costs etc. But the goal always was and will be to bootstrap as much as possible. Thus to successfully bootstrap we started a separate services department at Inator which soley focuses on providing complete web app solutions to our esteemed clients!
